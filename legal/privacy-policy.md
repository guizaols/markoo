# Privacy Policy (Draft)

Markoo processes bookmarks locally in the browser extension to provide search, organization, and management features.

## Data processed
- Bookmark metadata (title, URL, folder path).
- Local preferences (favorites, UI settings, entitlement flags).
- Optional product telemetry events.

## What we do not collect by default
- Full bookmark contents are not transmitted to external servers by default.
- No sale of personal data.

## User controls
- Users can remove extension data by uninstalling and clearing extension storage.
