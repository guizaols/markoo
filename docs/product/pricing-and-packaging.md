# Packaging Strategy (Free)

## Product model
Markoo is currently 100% free.

## Scope
- Full bookmark navigation and management features are available for all users.
- No paywall, no subscription, no billing flow.

## Why now
- Maximize adoption and retention.
- Focus team capacity on UX, performance, and reliability.

## Revisit condition
- Re-evaluate monetization only after product-market fit signals are stable.
