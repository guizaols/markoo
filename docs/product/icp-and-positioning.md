# Markoo ICP and Positioning

## ICP
- Primary: knowledge workers with 500-10,000 bookmarks (developers, PMs, founders, researchers).
- Secondary: students and analysts with heavy research workflows.

## Core pains
- Finding the right link is slow.
- Folders become hard to navigate at scale.
- Bookmark cleanup is manual and postponed.

## Positioning
- Category: professional bookmark workspace.
- Promise: find and organize bookmarks in seconds, even at large scale.
- Non-goal: generic social bookmarking or read-later product.

## Success criteria (90 days)
- Median search interaction under 150ms with 2,000 bookmarks.
- D30 retention >= 25% for activated users.
- Weekly active users growth trend is positive.
